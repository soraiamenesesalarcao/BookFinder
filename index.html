Plot

Wukong loves books. Unfortunately for him, a monster named Groucho decided to kidnapp him and split his books across a scary maze. Now he can only hope that his friend Molly makes it to the end of the maze while having collected his books in order to save him. Life won't be easy to Molly, though: she must reach the end within a time limit, collect the books and survive to the attacks from two kinds of enemies in the mean time.
